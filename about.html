GauRaksha is dedicated to the care and protection of cows in gaushalas. Our mission is to ensure that every cow receives the nourishment, shelter, and medical attention it deserves.

Through the support of compassionate donors and dedicated volunteers, we strive to create a safe and loving environment for these gentle beings, honoring their sacred place in our culture and heritage.
