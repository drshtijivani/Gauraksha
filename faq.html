FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GauRaksha?

GauRaksha is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting and caring for cows. We focus on providing shelter, healthcare, and raising awareness about the importance of cow protection.

How can I volunteer?

You can volunteer by visiting our Volunteer page and filling out the form. Our team will get in touch with you with available opportunities and guidance.

Where does my donation go?

Your donation helps us provide food, medical care, and shelter for cows in need. It also supports outreach and educational activities to promote cow welfare.

Can I visit the shelter?

Yes! We encourage visitors to come and see the work we do. Please contact us beforehand to schedule a visit.

Why is cow protection important in India?

Cow protection is considered vital in India due to cultural, religious, and ecological reasons. Cows are revered in Hinduism, and their preservation contributes to sustainable agriculture, dairy production, and biodiversity.

How can I report a cow in distress or illegal slaughter?

Please contact our 24/7 helpline given in blog or fill out the Report a Cow in Distress form on our website. We will dispatch our team to investigate and rescue the animal.
